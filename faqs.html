What is tsetse fly? Tsetse is a biting inset that transmits Trypanosomiasis to both humans and humans How do I know my animals has Trypanosomiasis
  • -when your livestock in a tsetse infested area

    • .Weight loss

    • .Pale mucus membrane

  • -Increased cost of treatment

Does Trypanosomiasis affect people? Yes It causes sleeping sickness Is sleeping sickness treatable? Yes Outcome largely depends on timely detection and appropriate treatment What is Pattec? Pan-African Tsetse and Trypanosomiasis Eradication Campaign (PATTEC) was formed following the African Head of State and Government in 2000 in Lome, Togo following a declaration (Decision AHG/156 ( XXXVI) of the 36th Assembly of Head of State and Government) .The Secretary General of the AU was charged with the task of initiating a campaign to eradicate the trypanosomiasis and tsetse flies menace from the African continent, once and for all. Since the launch of PATTEC, Tsetse and Trypanosomiasis problem has become more visible and all the affected countries have embarked on suppression to suppress with an ultimately eradication goal. The coordination office is based at African Union(AU) commission in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. What is Kenttec ? Successor to Pattec State Corporation mandated to steer/coordinate tsetse and trypanosomiasis eradication programs in the country. Kenttec has headquarters in Nairobi and five regional offices.
